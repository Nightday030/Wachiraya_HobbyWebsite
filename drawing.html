My Experience

I started drawing seriously about three years ago. At first, I mostly copied pictures and anime characters without really understanding what I was doing. It was fun, but progress felt slow.

After practicing more consistently, I began focusing on fundamentals like gesture, proportion, and basic shading. I still make mistakes, but I can clearly see improvement compared to when I first started. Drawing has taught me patience and how to enjoy the learning process instead of rushing the result.
